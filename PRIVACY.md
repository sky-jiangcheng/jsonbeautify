# Privacy Policy

**JSON Beautify Tool** is a local-first JSON formatting tool available on macOS, Windows, iOS, and the web. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Data Collection

We do **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal information or user content on external servers. All JSON input, formatting, and processing occur entirely on your own device. Nothing you type or paste is ever sent to us or to any third party.

## Local Storage

The app may store your formatting preferences (such as theme and indent settings) and any snippets you choose to save to history. This data is kept **locally on your device only**. It never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any external service.

## Third-Party Services

The app does **not** integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking SDKs. There are no network calls made by the core formatting features.

## Children

The app does not collect data from children and is suitable for users of all ages.

## Contact

For privacy-related questions, please open an issue on our GitHub repository:  
https://github.com/sky-jiangcheng/jsonbeautify/issues
